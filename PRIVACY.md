# Privacy Policy

Visa Sponsorship Checker processes LinkedIn job-card company names locally in the browser.

- No browsing history, job information or personal data is transmitted.
- No analytics, advertising SDKs, remote scripts or external APIs are used.
- The extension stores only the enabled/disabled state and selected country using `chrome.storage.local`.
- UK sponsor data is bundled with the extension and sourced from the official UK Visas and Immigration register.

Last updated: 27 June 2026.
