# Privacy Policy

Visa Sponsorship Checker processes LinkedIn job-card company names locally in the browser.

- No browsing history, job information or personal data is transmitted.
- No analytics, advertising SDKs or remote scripts are used.
- The extension stores the enabled/disabled state, selected country and locally cached sponsor-register data using `chrome.storage.local`.
- Sponsor data is bundled with the extension and sourced from official UK Visas and Immigration and IND registers.
- Once a week, the extension may download updated sponsor-register JSON files from this repository's GitHub raw file host. It does not send LinkedIn company names, job details or personal data with those requests.

Last updated: 28 June 2026.
